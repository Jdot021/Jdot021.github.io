About Me

About Us

My name is Jonathan Ford, and I am a current Senior at Auburn University, majoring in Computer Science.

Over the years, I have evolved into a confident and intelligent young man with passions that span video games, cars, music, and coding. In terms of automobiles, my interest ranges from learning the specs to actual hands-on fixing, and even simply enjoying drives during my free time. When it comes to music, I consider myself a versatile listener. I believe that embracing a wide variety of music broadens one's understanding of the numerous genres, exploring their differences and similarities. Coding is an interesting hobby as it's both challenging and rewarding. My intent is to continue practicing coding with hopes of becoming a Software Developer in the future. While developing code is fun, I find particular enjoyment in reviewing and enhancing already-written code. Similar to reverse engineering, I can deconstruct the flow of code and reproduce it or modify it to alter certain components. As a software developer, I aim to assist companies by troubleshooting and upgrading existing systems, as well as designing new systems, all while adhering to industry standards.
